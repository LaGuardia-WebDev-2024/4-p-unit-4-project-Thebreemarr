A Day In The Life Of Bree

The sunlight shines through the bushes outside my window and onto my face. I sit up and check my watch, it reads; 10:27. Its time for my morning walk on the beach.
I pull back the covers and slip my feet into my crocs.
Walking into my parents room I see my mom laying in bed, reading a book and petting my fluffy dog.
"Wanna go for a walk Toby?" I ask him. He jumps on me, wagging his tail and trying to lick my face. My mom laughs, "I think that means 'yes.'"
After my oatmeal breakfast, I check 'Surfline', my favorite surf app that tells me the quality of the waves.
It tells me 'poor to fair' from 9:00-12:00. I have to get out there soon before I miss my chance, I think.
I turn to my brother, Axel, who's eating a chocolate croissant next to me, "wanna go for a swim?," I ask him.
Just as me and Axel are walking back to the house, we run into my parents with the dog!
"We were thinking about taking the Kayaks out. Are you guys interested in coming with?"
"YES!" we tell them.
My dad helps me carry the surf board back home, we shower off, get dressed, and drive the boats down to the bay.
After Kayaking down the current for a few hours, we dock at the stretch of island called 'The Dike', and my mom goes to get the car.
We load the Kayaks onto the car and drive back to the house.
After a small snack, Axel and I decide to go for a bike ride to see the sunset. When we get back to the house, dinnner is ready! We have grilled salmon, potatoes, and squash; made on the outdoor grill.
After dinner we pack the car and drive back to NYC for another 5 days of school before Im back at the beach!
