Bree's Favorite Beaches

1. Long Beach Island, NJ 2. Cape Canaveral, FL 3. Cocoa Beach, FL 4. Jones Beach, NY
Long Beach Island is where Ive spent almost every weekend of my whole life. We have a little house by the beach that has been in the family for generations. The waves are great for swimming, boogie- boarding, surfing, etc. The sand is soft, the water is clear, the people are nice, and the vibes are flowing. I met my best friends at LBI, Adriana and Brooke. They visit less often then my family so im always looking forward to seeing them. Every February break for the past 5 years or so, my family has spent a week on the beaches of Cape Canaveral, (3 weeks during 2020). The waves are amazing; not rough at all but great for surfing. Part of the reason I love it so much is because it reminds me of Long Beach Island, my favorite place in the whole world. Before we discovered the wondrous Cape Canaveral, my family would stay in Cocoa Beach. Its another lovely place in Florida with amazing surf waves, a fun pier, and the world famous Ron John Surf Shop. Every year we rent bikes and ride down to the pier and surf shop. My favoite beach in New York City is Jones beach, because it usually has good waves and (imo) is one of the least polluted beaches in the city. I love to take my city friends to Jones Beach during the summer and its always really nice.
