Bree's Favorite Stores and Brands

My favorite stores on Long Beach Island include Ron Jon Surf Shop, Kitty Hawk Kites, Wave Hog, and Surf City 5&10.
I love the beachy aesthetics and the quality of their merchandise. I 100% recomend all of these stores! They are some of the best on the island.

My favorite resturants and cafes in LBI are Kubels, Agnello's, How You Brewin, Holiday Snackbar, and Panzones. Most of these places I discovered through my friends, Adriana and Brooke. We love going out to eat at Holiday Snackbar where we get Burgers or Panzones where we get Pizza. We also love going to How You Brewin or Agnello's to get bagels, sandwitches, fried chicken, cokes, and chai tea lattes.

My favorite places to go when im in Long Beach Island is the lighthouse, the Barnegat Light Playground, the 17th street Beach, or Fantasy Island (Amusement Park and arcade.) I recommend them all!
