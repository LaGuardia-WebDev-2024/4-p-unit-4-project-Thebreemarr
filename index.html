Bree's Favorite Beaches

1. Long Beach Island, NJ 2. Cape Canaveral, FL 3. Cocoa Beach, FL 4. Jones Beach, NY
"Long Beach Island is where Ive spent
almost every weekend of the whole year since I
was a kid. The waves are great for swimming, boogie-
boarding, surfing, etc. The sand is soft, the water is clear,
the people are nice, and the vibes are flowing. " 		Every February break for the past 5 years
or so, my family has spent a week on the beaches
of Cape Canaveral, (3 weeks during 2020).
The waves are amazing; not rough at all but
great for surfing. Part of the reason I love it
so much is because it reminds me of Long Beach Island,
my favorite place in the whole world.		 Before we discovered the wondrous
Cape Canaveral, my family would stay in
Cocoa Beach. Its another lovely
place in Florida with amazing surf waves,
a fun pier, and the world famous Ron John Surf Shop.
Every year we rent bikes and ride down to the pier
and surf shop. 		My favoite beach in New York City is
Jones beach, because it usually has good
waves and is one of the least polluted
beaches in the city.
